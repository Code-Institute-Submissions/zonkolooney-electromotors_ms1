Three Phase Motors

Three-phase motors are the most commonly used in industry, and since the arrival of the Inverter have almost obliterated Single-Phase motors. Single phase use is easily achievable on 230/400 Volt models with a single to three phase inverter

Single Phase Motors

Single-Phase motors ARE still available through Electromotors. They are available in both Capacitor Run style and Capacitor Start & Run style which are more suitable for higher torque requirements such as compressors, augers etc.

Frequency Inverters

Inverters have transformed the Electric Motor industry, allowing many people without 3 Phase power to use 3 Phase machines with a simple 1 Phase 13 Amp supply. MANY lathes, drills and similar machines have gotten new owners

Run Capacitors

Capacitors are items that commonly fail in modern appliance, due to overload and mechanical failure. We stock a full range of these items from 1uF to 100uF in Run Capacitors and many sizes in Start Capacitors to suit multiple mortors
